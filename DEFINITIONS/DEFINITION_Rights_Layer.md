# DEFINITION — Rights Layer

## Definition
The rights layer is the irreducible core: protections that cannot be optimized away, voted away by convenience, or bypassed by automation.

## Core properties
- Due process
- Equal protection
- Bodily autonomy
- Speech rights
- Property and contract integrity
- Right to appeal impactful decisions

## Rule
If an optimization conflicts with the rights layer, the optimization loses.
