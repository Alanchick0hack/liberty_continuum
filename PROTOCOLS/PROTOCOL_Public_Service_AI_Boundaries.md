# PROTOCOL — Public Service AI Boundaries

## Purpose
Define what AI may and may not do in governance-adjacent systems.

## Allowed
- Planning and simulation
- Resource allocation proposals
- Logistics routing
- Fraud detection signals
- Service triage and scheduling
- Drafting recommendations for human review

## Not allowed
- Punishment or sentencing decisions
- Coercive enforcement without human authority
- Silent surveillance or hidden scoring
- Identity fusion across services without consent
- Unlogged decision-making

## Required
- Decision logs for impactful actions
- Versioned system identity
- Appeal path
- Tripwires
